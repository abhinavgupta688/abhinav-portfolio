Sajha Platform

Zero-commission, agro-based equipment sharing platform.

THE PROBLEM

Small-scale Indian farmers struggle with high capital expenditures for farming machinery, often incurring massive debts to rent essential equipment.

THE SOLUTION

Sajha provides a peer-to-peer, zero-commission sharing model where local equipment owners list underutilized tractors and tools directly for rent, reducing farmer costs by up to 40%.

KEY CAPABILITIES

  • Zero-Commission Renting: Peer-to-peer equipment sharing model supporting COD and UPI payment options.
  • AI Crop Disease Diagnosis: Multi-modal scanner that analyses leaf images to identify crop pests and diseases.
  • AI Subsidy Finder & Advisory: Instantly checks eligibility for schemes like PM-KISAN & PMKSY.
  • Real-time Tracking & Voice Bookings: Leaflet mapping integration with live ETAs & multi-language voice guidance.
  • Kisan Chowpal: Collaborative local exchange forums & live scientist advisory video calls.
